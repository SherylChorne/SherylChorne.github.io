FAQs

Where was 2COOL founded?

2COOL was founded in Brooklyn, NY

When was 2COOL founded?

2COOL was founded in July, 2020

Who founded 2COOL?

Sheryl Chorne, a 16 year old from Brooklyn' NY, founded 2COOL with a vision of creating a revolutionary, comfortable, cooling, adjustable, and protective mask to the market. Until now, a product of this nature remains unprecedented.

What inspired you to come up with your product?

2COOL Face Masks was designed with the intention of making the mask-wearing process far more comfortable. We offer high quality 2 layer cotton face masks with a slit for our cooling inserts to keep your face sweat free while on the go. Our mask's adjustable features are perfect for the entire family, ensuring a perfect non - slip, snug fit for all.
