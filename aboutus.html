Founded in Brooklyn, NY, 2Cool Face Masks was designed with the intention of making the mask-wearing process far more comfortable. We offer high quality 2 layer cotton face masks with a slit for our cooling inserts to keep your face sweat free while on the go. Our mask's adjustable features are perfect for the entire family, ensuring a perfect non - slip, snug fit for all.
